Creative

I am a psychiatry researcher and data engineer living in Malden, MA. Outside of work, I enjoy hanging out with my cat Salad Dressing, scouring library used book sales, and making unnecessary spreadsheets. My writing explores neurodivergence, generative AI, and American history/politics. Distilled further, my writing orbits the central theme: miscommunication.
