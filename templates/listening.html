As a data engineer and an archaeologist by training, classification and curation are hobbies of mine. I have no musical ability of my own but I get a lot of joy out of sorting songs and curating playlists. My public playlist profile on Spotify is a wasteland that is impossible to navigate, so I created this page to highlight some of my personal favorites hidden in the mass of abandoned playlists. Hope you enjoy.

Monthly Playlists

Every month I create a new playlist for songs that I am listening to on repeat. It is usually moslty new songs I heard for the first time that month with the occassional song I love thrown in. Use the search below to see what I was listening to in any given month since February 2019.

Color Playlists

Out of boredom, I went through my liked songs and created separate playlists based on the color of their album art. In most cases I have tried avoiding adding multiple songs from the same album to any of the playlists (choosing just my favorite track instead).

TV Show Playlists

One of the things that will keep me invested in a TV show is good music. I have started making small playlists of songs I like from shows that I think had really solid soundtracks.
