Fiction

I am new to fiction writing, but have always been an avid reader. I am particularly drawn to emotionally rich and politically layered fiction. Some authors that have had the most direct impact on my own writing include Kurt Vonnegut, Jorge Luis Borges, Isabelle Allende, and Arthur Koestler. I am interested in themes of infinity, grief, etymology, lunacy, apparatus, classification, and time. To see what I have been reading lately, check out the Reading section of my website.
